Meet Mahi

Mahi founded OMG Gifts in 2023 with a mission to spread warmth through thoughtfully designed candles and gift hampers. Inspired by cosy evenings and the magic of small moments, she pours her passion into every creation.

When she’s not melting wax or curating goodies, you'll find her tending to plants, experimenting with new fragrances or writing handwritten notes for her customers.
