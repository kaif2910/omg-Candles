Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnaround time for orders?

Most orders are hand prepared and dispatched within 2–3 working days. Delivery within India typically takes an additional 3–5 days.

Do you offer bulk or corporate gifting?

Yes! We love helping businesses and events with custom gifting solutions. Please send us a message with your requirements and we’ll curate something special.

Can I customise scents or packaging?

Absolutely. Many of our products are made-to-order. Let us know your preferred fragrance, colour palette, or message and we’ll make it happen.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept secure UPI and net banking payments via WhatsApp checkout. Cash on delivery is currently unavailable.
